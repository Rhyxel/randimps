Terms of Service & Information

All owners of Randimps are covered under the following Terms of Service. By becoming the owner of a Randimp, you agree to these terms.

Code of Conduct

  1. Be civil to everyone, there is no need to be rude over Randimps!
  2. Once you own a Randimp, you are free to do with it what you'd like! Display them wherever (please link back to either the masterlist or the PCE thread)!
  3. To trade a or sell a Randimp, please make a post on the trading thread!
  4. If you no longer want your Randimps and want to give them away, consider releasing them back into the Gacha! (this will award you shinies, lusteries, and glowies!)

Items

  1. Items can be acquired via purchasing them with notes or essence fragments, releasing a Randimp back into the gacha, or from other players.
  2. Items can be traded to other players for PCE currency and items, Randimp currency and items (as well as Randimps), art, etc. Or you can gift them to people!
  3. Once purchased, items cannot be converted back into notes or essence fragments. No refunds, sorry!

Currency

  1. Shinies and Glowies can be purchased through the main thread with notes or EF, and can be purchased in bulk. Glowies can only be purchased using EF.
  2. Currencies can also be acquired by releasing a Randimp back into the wild! You will get currency corresponding to its rarity, and it will be added back into the Gacha.

Bait

  1. Bait can be used to acquire Randimps from the Gacha! It can be purchased using PCE currency or Randimps currency (S, G)
  2. Different kinds of bait will attract different rarities of Randimp. Normal bait can attract rarer Randimps, but the chances are much lower.
  3. Bait is not immediately used upon purchasing unless you specify that you want to immediately use it. This means you can keep bait in your inventory for later, either for a rainy day or to trade or give to other players.

Randimps

Randimps can be obtained through the following means:

  • Random Encounter Gacha
  • Events
  • Trading with other players

After you've obtained your Randimp, it will be kept up with on the masterlist. Please let me (Syntax) know if you would like to transfer or rename your Randimp!

Alteration Items

  1. Alteration items (like tokens) can be purchased with notes, EF, or Randimp currency (S, G)
  2. Alteration items can be applied to any Randimp you own to change their traits or appearance!
  3. Once used, the Randimp is permanently changed. If you would like to revert your Randimp, you would need to use another item.
